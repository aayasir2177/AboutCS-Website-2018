Hacking in CS 1.6

Wall Hacks
1.Simple Hack is very basic wall hack in this wall hack you can only see your friends and enemies.




2.In this wall hack the opacity is low of your map's walls, this is very useful type of wall hack not only you can see friends and enemies but also Boxes, other walls, Gates and many others things that against the walls.




3.as you can see in this hack you can see lines instead of walls ,Boxes, Gates and more.




4.This hack is just like low opacity hack, The difference is wall's opacity is lower than low opacity hack.



Smoke Hack
Smoke hack is a very useful hack for server players in this hack you can disable the smoke of smoke bomb.



Cursor Hack
The benefit of cursor hack is that suppose you're using Magnum you can also take shots when your Magnum is zoomed out.



Head Shot Hack
When you're using this hack your all the shots will be automatically changed into head shots, when you click Fire the aim will automatically goes to head.



Visibility Hack
Using this hack you can see your all enemies and friends as well, In the dark areas of your map. This will increase the brightness of players for increasing the visibility.
